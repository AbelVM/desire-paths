What this is:
Desire Paths is an interactive, open-source simulator that reveals where pedestrians naturally walk across real streets and parks. It uses agent-based modeling, H3 hex grids, and a walking-resistance surface to show why planned paths often fight human instinct.

Key pages:
- / — Main simulator (drop start/end points, generate pedestrian flow heatmaps)
- /docs/research.html — Research background and the CEUS 2025 paper "Predicting Desire Paths: Agent-Based Simulation for Neighbourhood Route Planning"
- /docs/faq.html — Frequently asked questions about methodology, data, and usage
